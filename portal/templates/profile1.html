Competetions

Team Robocon represents BITS Pilani in "ABU ROBOCON" held annually. It is an international Robotics Contest whose nationals are held in Pune.The problem statement involves building robots for accomplishing various challenging tasks which involve innovation in the fields of Design,Manufacturing,Electronics and Programming.The bots developed are either semi autonomous or fully autonomous. Besides this, The Team takes part in various Robotic Competitions held across the country during various times of the year. All the bots developed by the Team are put to display in Apogee, the annual technical festival of BITS,Pilani.
